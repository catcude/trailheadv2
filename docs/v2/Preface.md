# 00 — Preface

## To the Future Leaders of Trailhead

**This document is our North Star.**

A North Star doesn't tell you exactly where to step. It doesn't make difficult decisions for you, and it doesn't remove uncertainty. What it does is provide a constant point of reference when the path ahead becomes difficult to see.

We wrote these principles while our world was still quiet. There were no investors, no board, no users demanding features, no marketing team, no quarterly revenue targets, and no pressure to become something we were not. We chose to document these principles before there was pressure to compromise them.

These pages are not marketing, and they are not a promise of perfection. They are our best understanding of what we believe to be true about people, learning, growth, and the responsibility we carry in building Trailhead. As our understanding grows, these pages may grow with it. But they should never change simply because the world becomes louder.

If you ever find yourself wondering which direction to go, begin here. Return to these pages. Revisit the principles. Let them remind you not only of where Trailhead is going, but why it began in the first place.
